## What MathDeck Does

Enter a concept. Choose a subject. Get a complete, structured lecture deck in under 30 seconds.

Every deck includes:
- **Title + motivation slide** — opens with intuition and a central question
- **Formal definitions** — full LaTeX notation with plain-English restatements
- **Notation guide** — multiple representations labeled clearly
- **Visual examples** — step-by-step walkthroughs
- **Theorems** — stated precisely with proof sketches and significance
- **Exercises** — with complete solutions
- **Speaker scripts** — conversational, mathematically precise, ready to deliver

Export to **PDF, LaTeX, Markdown, or plain text**. Edit any slide inline. Share via URL.
