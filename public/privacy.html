Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 28, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 28, 2026

MathNest ("we", "us", or "our") operates mathnest.org, a tool for generating academic math lecture decks. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Account information. When you sign in with Google or GitHub via OAuth, we receive your email address, name, and profile picture from that provider. We do not receive or store your Google or GitHub password.

Usage data. We log each deck generation request, including the subject, concept entered, and whether the result was served from cache. This is used to enforce daily usage quotas and understand how the tool is used.

Deck content. Decks you save to your library are stored in our database linked to your account. Decks you share via URL are encoded entirely in the URL — we do not store shared decks on our servers.

Billing information. If you subscribe to a paid plan, payment is processed by Stripe. We store only your Stripe customer ID and subscription status — we never see or store your card details.

Technical data. We collect standard server logs including IP addresses, request timestamps, and HTTP status codes for security and debugging purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your session
  • To enforce daily generation quotas by plan tier
  • To sync your saved deck library across devices
  • To process subscription payments and verify plan status
  • To send you product update emails (only if you opt in)
  • To improve the product based on aggregate usage patterns

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with the third-party services required to operate MathNest:

  • Supabase — authentication, database (user accounts, decks, subscriptions)
  • Groq — AI generation (your concept input is sent to Groq's API to generate deck content)
  • Upstash Redis — caching generated decks and rate limit counters
  • Stripe — payment processing and subscription management
  • Vercel — hosting and serverless function execution
  • Cloudflare — DNS and DDoS protection
  • Userback — in-app feedback widget

Each provider has their own privacy policy and data processing terms.

4. Data Retention

Your account data and saved decks are retained as long as your account is active. Generated decks are cached for up to 7 days. If you delete your account, your data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser local storage to cache your deck library for offline access and to store your session token. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. Supabase uses a session cookie to keep you signed in.

6. Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export your saved decks (available in-app as JSON)
  • Opt out of product update emails at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at sarala10sh@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

MathNest is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Security

We use HTTPS for all data in transit. Authentication is handled by Supabase with OAuth tokens — we never store passwords. Stripe handles all payment data under PCI-DSS compliance. That said, no system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify signed-in users via an in-app notice.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at sarala10sh@gmail.com.
